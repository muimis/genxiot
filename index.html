1. Executive Summary & Product Overview

Genxiot LLP is pleased to present this Techno-Commercial Proposal for the Genxiot Alamo Smart Wireless Nurse Call System. Genxiot delivers modern healthcare automation across 100+ hospital facilities with over 10 Lakh nurse calls served.
