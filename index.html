Genxiot LLP  ·  Wireless Nurse Call System

We make hospitals safe and efficient.

Intelligent IoT solutions for modern healthcare. Real-time Nurse Calling, Smart Workflows, Actionable Insights, and Better Patient Outcomes.

Alamo Call Points

Battery-operated wall-mount solutions with Call, Service, Cancel, and Acknowledge options. Sleek design for new or operational hospitals.

Bed/Bathroom Accessories

Pendant Button: Coil-cord switch placed near the bed for convenient access.

Pull Cord: Easily accessible string cord designed for washroom safety.

Indicators & Displays

Room Indicators: Corridor LED lights that illuminate on call.
Corridor Displays: Pixel matrix boards for high visibility.
NS Monitors: Real-time Android/TV displays at nursing stations.

Gateways & Software

Standalone LoRa Gateways receive RF signals and route them to cloud/displays. The Alamo Software provides actionable insights, response-time tracking, and automated PDF reports for managers.

Dripo: Intelligent Bystander for IV Drips

Alerts errors in IV, helps set IV flow rates accurately, and provides data to analyze and improve processes for better patient safety.
